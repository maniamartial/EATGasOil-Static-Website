About Us

Energizing East Africa & beyond

With operations across the Region, East African Gasoil Ltd (EAGOL) is a privately owned Oil Marketing Company that was established in 2009 to Energize East Africa and Beyond! Our 10 years of operations have seen us continually meet and exceed our customer’s needs by providing quality petroleum products and exceptional service delivery

From a truck stop in Jomvu, Mombasa in the year 2010, EAGOL has since grown to an ISO certified Oil Marketing industry player with presence in 7 Counties in Kenya and across the Kenyan Boarders to Somalia, Uganda, South Sudan, and Congo. Our Head office in Mombasa oversees 6 retail outlets in Namanga, Kilifi, Eldoret, Kisumu, Nakuru and Kwale. Here we offer competitive prices for fuel products and go the extra mile with customer satisfaction on non-fuel products and services. We also provide efficient bulk fuel distribution from our depot stations in Mombasa, Eldoret, Nakuru, Kisumu and Nairobi with purchasing packages tailor made to suit our client’s needs.

Board of Directors

Mr. Hussein Ali

Chairman

Mr. Hussein Ali has a wealth of experience of over 20 years in the the field of humanitarian assistance and development. Having worked closely with regional, National, and international partners and authorities in the planning and implementation of humanitarian Assistance programmes of the International Committee of the Red Cross and the United States Agency for International Development in Somalia and Kenya. He holds a Masters of Science in Governance and Development Management from the University of Birmingham, England and a Masters Global Execution in Business Administration from United States International University for Africa (USIU-Nairobi). His focus is mainly on Governance, Development Management and Strategic leadership.
