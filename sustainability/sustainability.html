Co-oporate Social Responsibility

At EAGOL, we recognise our role as an economic organ of society and believe that tangible contribution towards building economic and societal enhancement of the community is commensurate to our overall performance. As we continue to grow as an Organization, we aim to promote impactful growth of the community around us.

Corporate Social Responsibility is an integral part of our business culture for which an annual budgetary allocation is provided. Our CSR policy outlines the areas in which we aim to make a positive difference for sustainable development through:

ROAD SAFETY

We endeavour to complement the existing national infrastructure and regulations for safe road use through initiatives that promote compliance. We have made it our purpose to equip road users with material that enhance their safety and the safety of their passengers.

EDUCATION

With education the backbone of development, we have set out to enhancing the community’s learning environment through activities that promote comfortable leaning and encourage intentional effort towards good performance

HEALTH

At EAGOL, a healthy community is as important as a healthy working environment. We seek to not only engage in activities that improve individual health and communal healthcare facilities, but we aim to promote implementation of the Occupational Health and Safety Act (OSHA) 2007 in the community as and where possible.

VULNERABLE GROUPS

Although our CSR policy outlines areas in which we strive to provide sustainable aid throughout the year, we have selected the month of Ramadhan to go the extra mile in helping the community with items of short and long term ease. These include but are not limited to sponsoring iftar programs and distribution of food and non-food items towards selected orphanages, revert centres and homes of the less privileged within the community

Quality Policy Statement

East African Gasoil Limited is committed to provide energy solutions comprising of petroleum products (Premium Motor Spirit(PMS),Jet A-1, Illuminating Kerosene(IK), Automotive Gas Oil(AGO) and Oils & Lubricants which meet and exceed customer requirements in accordance with the ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management Systems in line with the highest professional standards aiming for continual improvement and customer satisfaction through the involvement and participation of all levels of management, staff and other interested parties.

East African Gasoil Limited engages staff with the necessary competencies and shall always strive to provide training of staff involved in service realization to ensure that the customers always get the best. We will comply with all applicable statutory and regulatory requirements and will implement programs and procedures to assure compliance.

We will communicate our commitment to quality to our employees, suppliers, and customers. We will solicit their input in meeting our Quality goals and in turn will offer assistance to meet their goals.

The Management and staff of East African Gasoil Limited are committed to attaining the objectives provided by the framework of the quality policy and shall review the quality policy and the objectives regularly to ensure continued suitability.

Environment Policy

By design, our business has an inevitable impact on the environment. Because we at EAGOL are conscious of this, we are committed to conduct our operations in an environmentally friendly and sustainable manner. We constantly and continually dedicate intentional effort towards the improvement of our operational performance in order to reduce possible negative impact on the environment In line with our vision to be a Regional leader in the Oil Industry, we have taken the onus to play our part in safeguarding our natural surroundings through responsible reduction of waste and pollution. This we strive to achieve by continuously:

  • Complying with the regulatory standards set by the National Environmental Management Authority and maintaining international environmentally friendly best practices in the Oil Industry
  • Participating in preventive identification and evaluation of environmental hazards within our operations and actively working towards adopting measures to eliminate or control them
  • Promoting awareness, accountability and responsibility on environmental matters among our employees, customers, partners, surrounding communities and industry stakeholders.
  • Including protection of the environment as a priority and critical factor in all our business decisions

Guiding Principle

EAGOL is a provider of fuel that is important to driving progress and improving the lives of people around the East African Region. EAGOL acknowledges the responsibilities that it has to its neighbors, customers, communities and shareholders in its daily operations as it finds safe, efficient and responsible ways to bring this fuel to the market. The principles that guide EAGOL in these processes are:

Shareholders

EAGOL is committed to enhancing the long-term value of the investments entrusted to it by the shareholders by running the business profitably and responsibly maintaining a high level of good corporate governance.

Customers

EAGOL’s success depends on the ability to consistently satisfy the ever changing Customer preferences. It is committed to being innovative and responsive, while offering high quality products and services at competitive prices.

Employees

EAGOL has an exceptional quality of workforce providing a valuable competitive Edge. It strives to hire and retain the most qualified people available and is committed to the professional development and supporting their career goals through training.

EAGOL is committed to maintaining a safe working environment for our employees enriched by diversity and characterized by open communication, trust, and fair treatment.

communities

EAGOLcommits to being a good corporate citizen in all the places it operates. EAGOL Maintains high ethical standards, is fully compliant with all applicable laws, rules and regulations, and respects local and national cultures in areas where it operates.

EAGOL is dedicated to running safe and environmentally responsible operations
